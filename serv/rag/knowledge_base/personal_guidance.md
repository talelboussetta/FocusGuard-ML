# Personal Guidance

## Study Consistency
Consistency beats intensity.
Small daily effort is better than cramming.

---

## Motivation
Motivation follows action, not the opposite.
Start small to build momentum.

---

## Burnout Prevention
Signs:
- Constant fatigue
- Loss of motivation
- Irritability

Prevention:
- Rest
- Physical activity
- Boundaries
- Hobbies
- Social connections
- Professional help if needed
---
